About Manny

Hey there! I’m Manny, the founder, editor, and the driving force behind MannyMaker

Ever since I can remember, I’ve been captivated by the world of crafts. From colorful paintings to intricate sculptures, I’ve always found joy in creating something from scratch. Now, I’m thrilled to share my love for crafts with you through my website.

It all started when I was a kid, tinkering around with paints, clay, and whatever materials I could get my hands on. There was this unexplainable joy that came from bringing something to life with my own two hands. Little did I know that this spark of creativity would shape my entire future.

Over time, I realized that my creations had the power to touch people’s lives. As I had bought Cricut and Silhouette machines, friends and family started coming to me for advice regarding new craft ideas.

That’s when the idea of Mannymaker.com was born. It became my mission to create a platform where I could showcase my designs, share my knowledge, and build a community of like-minded individuals who shared my passion for creativity. I poured my heart and soul into creating a space that would inspire, support, and celebrate the artistic journeys of others.

Launching Mannymaker was a dream come true. It’s not just a business venture for me; it’s a way of life. Every design, tutorial, and resource on the website is a reflection of my own experiences, my love for crafts, and my desire to empower others to discover the joy of creating.

Welcome to Mannymaker.com, where creativity knows no bounds!
